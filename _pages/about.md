I am a GenAI Solution Architect at Google and a Ph.D. Scholar in Machine Learning at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. My work lies at the intersection of advanced artificial intelligence research and enterprise-scale architectural deployment. 

Currently, my academic research focuses on Large Language Models, Multimodal In-Context Learning, and developing robust few-shot prompting strategies for unseen tasks. I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of Spoken Language Understanding (SLU) and Agentic Frameworks.

In my professional capacity at Google, I lead Generative AI strategies and architect complex multi-agent systems using Google ADK to solve enterprise-level challenges. Prior to this, I drove major data science and AI initiatives across various industries, including Walmart, Honeywell, and GE Global Research.

I am driven by the ability to bridge the gap between cutting-edge ML research and strategic, long-term organizational impact.
